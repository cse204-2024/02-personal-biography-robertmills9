LeBron James

Biography

Early Life: LeBron James was born on December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio. He attended St. Vincent-St. Mary High School, where he gained national attention for his basketball skills.

Career

LeBron James, widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players, was selected first overall in the 2003 NBA Draft by the Cleveland Cavaliers. His career includes multiple NBA championships, MVP Awards, and All-Star selections, playing for the Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and the Los Angeles Lakers. His impact extends off the court, with significant philanthropic efforts and business ventures.
